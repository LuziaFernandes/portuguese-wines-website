Small Introduction...

Portuguese wines are a combinitation of cultural heritage from ancient civilizations, from the Phoenicians, Carthaginians, Greeks and specially the Romains. Portugal is a small contry but concentrates a great range of different climas and grapes. This results in a great variety of wines, with different profiles - since the hight altitudes freshness to the hot plains lands full body wines. Also, different tecniques are use from ancient methods resulting in diferent type of wines, like the Vinho Verde, the wine from Colares, the Moscatel, and on and on.
